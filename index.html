From Seoul to the World

Born March 20, 1967 in Seoul, South Korea, Richard came to America at age 4 and grew up in Cerritos, California. From humble beginnings, he built a life marked by boundless energy, an infectious laugh, and a magnanimous personality that touched everyone he met.

The Hospitality Dream

At 19, Richard started in the mailroom at Hilton Anaheim. This sparked a 17-year career that took him from earning his Cornell hospitality certification to becoming one of the youngest Hotel Managers at Hilton. He rose through determination, sharp business sense, and genuine care for people.

The Entrepreneur's Leap

In 2002, Richard took a leap of faith. He transformed La Casa del Camino in Laguna Beach, creating the Rooftop Lounge—the first rooftop bar in Laguna Beach. Born an entrepreneur, Richard became his own boss and grew his business empire through vision, hard work, and that magnetic personality.
